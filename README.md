## üöÄ The Mission
The gap between "Technical Reality" and "Business Strategy" is where security budgets die. **OCH CyberLingua** is a hybrid-AI application designed to fix this. 

It translates complex technical risks (like Agentic AI or Non-Human Identity) into the business language of Value, Impact, and Action.

## üß† The V.I.S.A. Framework
This engine is built upon the proprietary **V.I.S.A. Framework** for executive communication:

* **V**alue: What revenue stream or asset are we protecting?
* **I**mpact: The 4-Scenario Business Loss (Shutdown, Cleanup, Penalty, Exodus).
* **S**tory: A physical-world analogy that kills jargon.
* **A**ction: The specific strategic ask.
